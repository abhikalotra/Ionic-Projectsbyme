Ask and you shall be answered (FAQs)
  1. Is there a membership fee for this program?
    There is NO membership fee for the Diesel Cult.
  2. How long is membership valid?
    Membership is valid for three years from the date of enrolment. To know more about your membership, log in to the Cult App or check at your nearest Diesel Store.
  3. What is the procedure for acquiring the exclusive benefits and privileges against my membership?

    • For redemption of Style Miles on special Cult merchandise, simply log on to your Diesel Cult app.
    • You can visit our store and just tell them your registered mobile number or Cult ID for any kind of special assistance at the store.
    • You can call in to book an appointment with your stylist or personal shopper a day in advance. You can request the blocking of a trial room at least two hours before your arrival.
    • Bookings for the VIP room at Juhu and After hours Shopping can be requested for 24 hours in advance.
    • The booking will be for the appointment day only. It will become null and void the next day.
    • To make all your exclusive bookings and appointments, please call the respective store numbers.
    • Alternatively, a mail can be dropped at customercare_dieselindia@diesel.com with your request.
  4. From when will the Style Miles get calculated?
    The Style Miles are calculated from the day you become a Classic or Higher Cult member.
  5. Can I exchange/return any Diesel merchandise which I have bought with my style miles?
    No. The merchandise redeemed against Style Miles cannot be exchanged or returned.
  6. Can I redeem my Style Miles for cash?
    No. Style Miles cannot be redeemed for cash. They can only be redeemed for Diesel merchandise from the Cult Catalogue.
  7. How can I check my current style miles?

    The Style Miles can be checked:
    • On your Diesel Cult app
    • At the store: Just provide your Cult ID or the registered mobile number and get your Style Miles statement
    • Email: You can also email us at customercare_dieselindia@diesel.com
  8. How soon will style miles get updated to my account?
    The Style Miles get updated to your account within 48 hours of your last purchase.
  9. How can I communicate and change in my address or phone number?
    You can update the same in the ‘My Profile’ section of the Diesel Cult app or email us at customercare_dieselindia@diesel.com
  10. How can I get in touch with customer care for more queries regarding my membership?
    You can email us at customercare_dieselindia@diesel.com. Our Cult executive will call you back for assistance.
