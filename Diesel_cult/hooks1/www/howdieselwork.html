How diesel works?

  • Classic Cult

    Consider it your reward for being bad. Simply squander Rs. 25,000 at any Diesel store (at once, or over multiple visits) to gain entry into the Classic Cult.

    Once in, here is what you can expect:

    • Cool limited-edition merchandise
    • Exclusive access to sales
    • New collection previews
    • Invites to exclusive Diesel Events
    • Style mile bonuses on special occasions
    • Special discounts
    Classis Cult members can move to the Higher Cult with a total purchase of Rs. 75000 within one year of membership.

  • Higher Cult

    A collective purchase of Rs. 75,000 (at once, or over multiple visits) opens the gates to the inner sanctum of indulgence. With access to the Higher Cult, members can expect privileges and services designed to slake their vast and immeasurable desires.

    • Cool limited-edition merchandise
    • Exclusive access to sales
    • New collection previews
    • Invites to exclusive Diesel Events
    • Style mile bonuses on special occasions
    • Special discounts
    • Personal shopping assistant
    • Private stylist
    • VIP room access (Juhu)
    • After hours shopping
    • Diesel at your doorstep

  • Earning style miles

    You must earn redemption before you can attain it.

    The opportunities to score Style Miles are aplenty. You just need to be seen, preferably with a shopping bag in tow. On every transaction made at the Diesel store during the program, you earn precious Style Miles. These get credited to your account and are stored away for future redemption.

    Style Miles get credited to your account, depending on how high up in the divine hierarchy you are. Classic Cult members earn 15 Style Miles for every purchase worth Rs. 1000. Higher Cult members earn 30 Style Miles for every Rs. 1000 spent.

  • Redeeming Style Miles

    Let us lead you down the path of redemption and fulfil your deepest materialistic desires

    As a Classic or Higher Cult member, hoard 1500 Style Miles and redemption will be well within your grasp.

    All it takes is a visit to your nearest Diesel Store or logging in to the Cult App to start redeeming your Style Miles.


    The Cult promises to deliver your Diesel merchandise to your den so that you can celebrate attainment in private. However, if you’d rather flaunt your stuff, you’re welcome at the store. Delivery of merchandise could take up to three weeks depending on availability and distance. But that’s the price of redemption.

  • Your stay with the Cult

    The Cult Membership (for both Classic and Higher Cult) is valid for a period of three years.

    All the accrued Style Miles need to be redeemed within a period of three years.

    To ensure you get the most out of your Style Miles, Cult will extend your redemption period for another three months.

    Your path to redemption is now open!
